Class 10 board exams to be conducted in class 11, says Minister

Staff Reporter
CHENNAI, March 31, 2021 12:06 IST
Updated: March 31, 2021 12:06 IST
Staff Reporter
CHENNAI, May 12, 2020 12:06 IST
Updated: May 12, 2020 12:06 IST

The exams were supposed to have begun on March 27, but were postponed due to the COVID-19 lockdown

    Share Article
  • PRINT
  • A A A

A file photograph from the 2019 class 10 exams in Chennai. Photograph used for representational purposes only   | Photo Credit: M. PRABHU

The class 10 board exams which were supposed to be conducted in May this year are cancelled and for the first time board will be conducted for class 11 in session 2021-22

Class 10 board exams which were to commence from may this year are cancelled and students will be promoted to class 11 without any exams however students will be awarded grades on the basis on their performance in the current academic session,

The students are free to chose any stream and even students who opted for basic maths can chose mathematics added Mr.Pokhriyal.

Students and parents welcomed this move keeping in mind the spike in the daily Covid-19 infection count

The students who want to opt for science are concenred that this move dratically reduce their percentage. Teachers also echoed the cries of science students and described this move are diappointng. Although it shouldl be noted that today is fools day
